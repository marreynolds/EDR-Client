Welcome to Event Driven Results!

Here at Event Driven Results, We believe that every individual should be working towards some sort of fitness goal. This may be hard to achieve because of lack of knowledge or lack of motivation. That's why we created the perfect app to provide not only clear and concise milestones to achieve every two weeks, but also a way to connect you with local events and people in your area! Whether you're training to run the Boston Marathon or are just trying to find a local running group Event Driven Results wants you to pursue your goals and make meaningfull connections in your local community!
